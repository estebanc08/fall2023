Brute Forcing Strategy
    * Brute Forcing for large collections of salted passwords is pretty much hopeless in todays environment
    * May use quantum computing, but dont hold your breath
    * For now, the drill is:
        - If youre going to have to brute force one of a number of accounts, choose the one most likely to have uncomplicated accounts
        - Follow the statistics for password lengths for doing your tries (increasing probability you will finish early)

Dont deploy using default passwords
    * Motorola/Arris Surfboard Modem
    * Mirai botnet DDOS attacks of 2018
